A Little About Me

Hi, I'm James Lett, an African American paper artist with a love for bold colors and playful compositions. My illustrations come to life through the tactile process of paper cutting, where handcrafted textures meet the simplicity of bold shapes.

Inspired by the bold simplicity of mid-century poster designs and the imaginative spirit of children's books, my paper art illustrations bring playful energy to stories and worlds. Through a tactile, layered process, reminiscent in its accessible imagery and vibrant palettes of the iconic work of Eric Carle, I aim to create pieces that spark wonder and joy, celebrating the beauty of the perfectly imperfect handmade touch.
